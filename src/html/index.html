🎮 How to Play Minesweeper

👆
Left Click: Reveal a cell
🚩
Right Click: Flag/unflag a cell
🎯
Goal: Find all mines without clicking on them
🔢
Numbers: Show how many mines are adjacent

🤖 AI Assistant Guide

🧠 None: Play manually without AI assistance
😊 Easy AI: Makes random safe moves - good for beginners
🤔 Medium AI: Uses basic logic and probability
🤖 Hard AI: Advanced algorithms with pattern recognition

💡 AI Tips:

  • Select AI difficulty and click "AI Move" to let it play
  • You can switch between manual and AI moves anytime
  • Use AI to get unstuck when you're not sure what to do
